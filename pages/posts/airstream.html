The previous site had not been built with flexibility in mind. As a result, the wheel kept getting reinvented for each new piece of content and before long, it was a Frankensite. The redesign was all about making the site more usable, achieving a holistic look and feel, and making the content more manageable. To accomplish the last, we used [TypeRocket](https://typerocket.com/) which allowed us to apply [Laravel’s](https://laravel.com/) MVC concepts to a WordPress theme.
