Airstream

I was fortunate enough to help build the new website for Airstream, manufacturer of the iconic “silver bullet” travel trailer.

The previous site had not been built with flexibility in mind. As a result, the wheel kept getting reinvented for each new piece of content and before long, it was a Frankensite. The redesign was all about making the site more usable, achieving a holistic look and feel, and making the content more manageable. To accomplish the last, we used TypeRocket which allowed us to apply Laravel’s MVC concepts to a WordPress theme.
